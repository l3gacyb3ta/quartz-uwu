## Securing Data
---

Data sitting on your computer or on a portable medium
Attackers need accesss to the storage medium, be it remote or not

Assumption:
- Attacker has access to the storage medium
- Attacker does not have access to the data
Who cares:
- Shared computers
- Laptops
- SMARTPHONES
- Data on portable media
Mitigation:
- Make data local only
- Backups may be an issue
